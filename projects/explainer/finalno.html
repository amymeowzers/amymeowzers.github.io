JUST SAY NO: It can be embarrassing to say “no”, and you may feel as if you’re letting the other person down. When this happens, remember that your time is your most precious commodity. As much as possible, spend it on activities that meet your needs, make you happy, or advance your goals.

When someone invites you to some activity that doesn’t fit these criteria, you’re entitled to say “no” without explanation. Just say, "Sorry, I’m not available." Firmly declining, yet staying polite.

REMEMBER: Rejecting an invitation doesn’t mean you’re rejecting the person who sent it. It’s just a statement that you can’t attend.

You may feel bad in the moment, but it’s literally fine. It’s not that deep. Don’t overthink it, and remember to value your own time.
